Trail Mix Fan Page

  1. "Trail mix" is the term for a mix of bite-sized ingredients that travels well.
  2. Hikers, campers and parents of young children like trail mix because it's lightweight and portable.
  3. Whatever you call it, trail mix typically includes nuts, seeds and dried fruit tossed together without cooking.
  4. It's perfect travel food because no refrigeration is necessary.
